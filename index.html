Geology

Introducing the wonders of Earth

Have you ever wondered about the formation of rocks and crystals? They are some of the most beautiful parts of nature on Earth. Gemstones and crystals are usually found in stream and gravel beds of dried rivers Gemstones are usually created by great pressure and heat.

Gemstones Found In Nature

Why Are They Rare?

Gemstones and crystals make up only 1% of the Earth's substance. This means that these marvels are rarely found because they are formed below the Earth's surface, and are brought up to the surface of Earth by explosions of molten rock.
